For Visitors - Pond Springs Cemetery

Visitors, please:

In the spirit of a Benjamin Franklin quote, “One can tell the morals of their culture by the way they treat their dead”:

  • BE CAREFUL. No climbing on the stones, they may topple. Stay clear of them. There are also holes in the ground.
  • Pond Springs Historical Cemetery Association, Inc. is in charge of the site.
  • Respect the historical significance of this cemetery.
